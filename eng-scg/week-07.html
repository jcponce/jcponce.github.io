Week 7 - Matrices

Augmented matrix system - solving a set of equations
  • Recall the system of equations that we considered last week: \[ \begin{array}{r} x& +&y&+&z&=&4 \\ x& -&y&-&2z &=& 1 \\ 2x&+& y& -&z &=& 2 \\ \end{array} \]
  • Rewrite as a matrix equation:

    \(A x = \) \( \left( \begin{array}{r} 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 & -2 \\ 2 & 1 & -1 \\ \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ \end{array} \right) \) \( = a \)

Augmented matrix system - solving a set of equations
  • Rewrite as a matrix equation:

    \(A x = \) \( \left( \begin{array}{rrr} 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 & -2 \\ 2 & 1 & -1 \\ \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{r} 4 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ \end{array} \right) \) \( = a \)

  • Augmented form:

    \( \left( \begin{array}{r|r} A & a \end{array} \right) \) = \( \left( \begin{array}{rrr|r} 1 & 1 & 1 & 4\\ 1 & -1 & -2 & 2\\ 2 & 1 & -1 & 1 \\ \end{array} \right) \)

Week 7 - Matrices

Augmented matrix system - solving a set of equations

- Recall the system of equations that we considered last week: \[ \begin{array}{r} x& +&y&+&z&=&4 \\ x& -&y&-&2z &=& 1 \\ 2x&+& y& -&z &=& 2 \\ \end{array} \]

- Rewrite as a matrix equation:

\(A \mathbf x = \) \( \left( \begin{array}{r} 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 & -2 \\ 2 & 1 & -1 \\ \end{array} \right) \) \( \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \\ z \\ \end{array} \right) \) \( = \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ \end{array} \right) \) \( = \mathbf a \)


Augmented matrix system - solving a set of equations

- Rewrite as a matrix equation:

\(A \mathbf x = \) \( \left( \begin{array}{r} 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 & -2 \\ 2 & 1 & -1 \\ \end{array} \right) \) \( \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \\ z \\ \end{array} \right) \) \( = \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ \end{array} \right) \) \( = \mathbf a \)

- Augmented form:

\( \left( \begin{array}{r|r} A & \mathbf a \end{array} \right) \) = \( \left( \begin{array}{r} \\ \\ \\ \end{array} \right. \) \( \left. \begin{array}{rrr|} 1 & 1 & 1 \;\;\\ 1 & -1 & -2\;\; \\ 2 & 1 & -1 \;\; \\ \end{array} \right. \) \( \left. \begin{array}{r} \;4\\ \;1\\ \;2 \\ \end{array} \right. \) \( \left. \begin{array}{r} \\ \\ \\ \end{array} \right) \)



Augmented matrix system - solving a set of equations

\( \left( \begin{array}{r|r} A & \mathbf a \end{array} \right) \) = \( \left( \begin{array}{r} \\ \\ \\ \end{array} \right. \) \( \left. \begin{array}{rrr|} 1 & 1 & 1 \;\;\\ 1 & -1 & -2\;\; \\ 2 & 1 & -1 \;\; \\ \end{array} \right. \) \( \left. \begin{array}{r} \;4\\ \;1\\ \;2 \\ \end{array} \right. \) \( \left. \begin{array}{r} \\ \\ \\ \end{array} \right) \)

- We can perform a method of elimination by

  • Swapping,
  • scaling, and/or
  • adding rows together.

This method is known as row reduction, Gaussian elimination, Gauss-Jordan elimination.

Linear Algebra

Some key points to focus on

  1. Understanding the concept of a vector space and being able to manipulate vectors and vector spaces.
  2. Mastering the basics of linear transformations and their properties, such as the preservation of vector space structure and the ability to be represented by matrices.
  3. Learning how to work with matrices and solve systems of linear equations using matrix operations.
  4. Understanding the concept of eigenvalues and eigenvectors and being able to compute them for linear transformations.
  5. Developing a familiarity with the applications of linear algebra in various fields, such as engineering, physics, and computer science.
